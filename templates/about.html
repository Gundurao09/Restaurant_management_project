Restaurant Management System

Order Management

  • Order Taking: Allows servers to take customer orders digitally, reducing the reliance on paper menus.
  • Order Tracking: Tracks the status of orders from placement to delivery.
  • Table Management: Manages table assignments, reservations, and occupancy status.

Menu Management

  • Menu Customization: Enables easy modification of menus, including adding, updating, or removing items.
  • Pricing and Discounts: Allows setting prices, discounts, and special offers for menu items.
